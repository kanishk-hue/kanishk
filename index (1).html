The Mission

Serious Gear for
Serious Athletes

FitBulls was built in a 600-square-foot garage in 2020 by two brothers tired of equipment that bent, stripped, or quit under real load. They wanted gear as relentless as their training.

Today we partner with foundries that supply professional sports franchises, and torture-test every product before it ships. No shortcuts. No compromises.

FitBulls now equips home gym warriors, competitive powerlifters, CrossFit boxes, and D1 athletic programs across North America — all built on the same obsession that started in that garage.
